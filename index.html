About Aarogyam

Advanced Care Rooted in Compassion

Founded on the guiding belief that "Loving everything about oneself is the beginning of healing," Aarogyam Hospital represents a lifetime of medical dedication. Established by the esteemed team of Dr. Sunil Sarode and Dr. Shamalee Sarode, our institution brings over three decades of clinical expertise to safeguard and improve mother and newborn health.

We are deeply committed to providing comprehensive care while raising awareness for the prevention of feminine diseases. By combining a comforting family-centric environment with state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic facilities, we ensure that every woman and child receives top-tier treatment tailored to their needs.
