What We Have

Our Infrastructure

Every facility needed for safe, comprehensive care in one location.

ICU — Intensive Care Unit

24/7 monitored critical care with ventilator support and specialist on call at all times.

NICU — Neonatal ICU

Specialised care for premature and critically ill newborns with incubators and 24/7 monitoring.

PICU — Paediatric ICU

Dedicated intensive care for critically ill children led by our Paediatric Intensivist.

Operation Theatre

Fully equipped modular OT with laparoscopic setup and advanced anaesthesia support.

Sonography Unit

High-resolution digital ultrasound for obstetric, gynaecological and abdominal imaging.

Pathology Laboratory

In-house diagnostic lab with fast, accurate results for all routine investigations.

Emergency Ward

24/7 emergency care with immediate response for critical and urgent medical situations.

Inpatient Rooms

Comfortable, clean private and shared rooms for recovering patients and their families.
