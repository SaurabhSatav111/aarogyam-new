The Aarogyam Legacy

Advanced Mother & Child Care Rooted in Empathy

Established with a guiding philosophy that "Loving everything about oneself is the beginning of healing," Aarogyam Hospital has been a sanctuary of clinical trust and recovery. Our journey is one of passionate dedication, led by the distinguished couple of Dr. Sunil Sarode and Dr. Shamalee Sarode, who have spent over three decades providing clinical expertise to safeguard mother and newborn health.

What started as a focused clinic has now expanded into a leading superspeciality hospital at CIDCO N-11, Sambhajinagar. Our primary goal remains unchanged: providing high-quality, comprehensive maternal and paediatric care in a safe, welcoming, family-focused environment. We believe in taking medicine beyond the prescription, focusing on patient education, support, and the preventative healthcare of women and children.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted hospital for Mother & Child care, celebrated for clinical safety, innovation, and humane care standards.

Our Mission

To provide premium healthcare across gynaecology, neonatology, and paediatrics through state-of-the-art infrastructure and personalized attention.

Our Core Pillars

The Three E's of Aarogyam

How we define and deliver healthcare that transforms lives.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest clinical standards of healthcare. From Level-III Neonatal Intensive Care (NICU) and Pediatric ICU (PICU) to modular operating theatres, we deploy medical precision and advanced technology for every emergency and standard procedure.

  • Paediatric Asthma Guidelines
  • Nutrition & Vaccination Education

Empowerment

We believe every woman should be an active, informed participant in her healthcare journey. We empower women with preventive feminine healthcare, regular screening packages, family planning counseling, and safe, dignified maternity paths.

  • Preventive Screening Programs
  • Gynaecology Cancer Screenings
  • Family-Centered Maternity Paths
Our Medical Directors

The Visionaries Behind Aarogyam

Meet the highly distinguished clinical specialists leading our hospital.

Director - Woman Care

Dr. Shamalee Sunil Sarode

MBBS, DGO (Obstetrics & Gynaecology)
15+ Years of Clinical Practice

Dr. Shamalee Sarode is a highly esteemed Obstetrician and Gynaecologist. She is dedicated to offering comprehensive healthcare services to women of all ages. Her expertise spans high-risk maternity management, painless labor techniques, laparoscopic gynaecological procedures, and early detection and screening for feminine cancers.

She is deeply passionate about public health, regularly conducting awareness programs on feminine hygiene, menstrual health, and maternal wellness for adolescent girls and rural communities.

"Caring for a woman means caring for an entire household. Our goal is to bring confidence, security, and the highest standards of safety to every delivery."
High-Risk Obstetrics Laparoscopy Surgery Feminine Cancer Care Infertility Care
Director - Child Care

Dr. Sunil Eknath Sarode

MBBS, DCH (Diploma in Child Health)
15+ Years of Clinical Practice

Dr. Sunil Sarode is a celebrated Paediatrician, Neonatologist, and Pediatric Pulmonology specialist. He is the clinical head of Aarogyam's advanced level-III Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU). Dr. Sarode is widely recognized for his clinical management of critical newborns, childhood asthma, growth disorders, and developmental delay.

His systematic and patient-first approach to pediatric care has earned the trust of thousands of families across Sambhajinagar. He is also a key advocate for pediatric vaccination schedules and nutrition.

"Every child deserves the chance to grow up healthy and strong. Our NICU and pediatric services are built to give critical newborns the best chance at a beautiful life."
Neonatology Intensive Care Pediatric Pulmonology Child Asthma Specialist Developmental Monitoring
Our Milestones

The Aarogyam Journey

A look at how we grew into a beacon of hope and medical excellence.

1996

The Foundation Clinic

Dr. Sunil and Dr. Shamalee Sarode establish a local clinic in Sambhajinagar, laying down the core values of dedicated, patient-first care for mothers and children.

2006

Expansion of Specialist Services

Introducing dedicated neonatology and advanced gynaecological treatment, filling a vital gap in specialist child care in the CIDCO area.

2016

Inauguration of CIDCO N-11 Hospital

Moving into our custom-built, modern superspeciality facility. We deployed a Level-III NICU, advanced PICU, and modular operating theatres to handle complex medical scenarios.

2021

10,000+ Happy Deliveries

Celebrating the birth of over 10,000 babies and securing our position as the premier trusted family choice for maternity and infant critical care in the district.

Present

Cashless & Comprehensive Services

Operating a fully integrated hospital with 30+ insurance/TPA cashless partners, 24/7 emergency response, modern laboratory services, and continuous clinical innovation.
