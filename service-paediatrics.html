Paediatrics

Comprehensive child healthcare from newborn through adolescence, led by Dr. Sunil Sarode, MD Pediatrics, DNB, MNAMS.

Book Appointment Call Now

About This Service

Our paediatric department provides complete healthcare for children — from routine vaccinations and growth monitoring to diagnosis and treatment of childhood illnesses. Dr. Sunil Sarode leads the team with warm, parent-friendly guidance to monitor child milestones and keep young lives on track with optimal development and health.

Who It's For

Children from newborn through adolescence needing routine check-ups, vaccinations or treatment for illness.

What to Expect

A child-friendly environment, thorough examination and clear guidance for parents on care at home.

How It Works

1

Check-up & History

Growth assessment and discussion of any symptoms or concerns.

2

Diagnosis

Examination and any needed tests to identify the condition.

3

Treatment & Follow-up

Clear treatment plan with follow-up scheduled as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should my child have check-ups?

Regular well-child visits are recommended — more frequently in the first 2 years, then annually.

Do you provide vaccinations?

Yes, we follow the complete recommended immunisation schedule for children.

What if my child has a fever at night?

Our 24/7 emergency line is always available for urgent paediatric concerns.
