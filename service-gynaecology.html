Gynaecology

Expert diagnosis and treatment of female reproductive health conditions including menstrual disorders, PCOS, fibroids and infections across all life stages.

Book Appointment Call Now

About This Service

Our gynaecology department provides comprehensive care for reproductive health — from adolescence through menopause. Led by Dr. Shamalee Sarode, MS-OBGY, DNB, we diagnose and treat the full range of gynaecological conditions. We specialize in minimally invasive solutions, routine screenings, and long-term care management for chronic issues, supporting women at every milestone of their life journey.

Who It's For

Women of all ages experiencing menstrual irregularities, pelvic pain, infections, PCOS or other reproductive health concerns.

What to Expect

A thorough consultation, examination and personalised treatment plan discussed clearly with you.

How It Works

1

Consultation

Discuss your symptoms and medical history in a private, comfortable setting.

2

Examination & Tests

Physical examination and any required blood tests or ultrasound.

3

Treatment Plan

Personalised medication, procedure or lifestyle plan explained in detail.

Frequently Asked Questions

At what age should I start seeing a gynaecologist?

Generally from the first menstrual period, or earlier if you have specific concerns.

Is a gynaecological exam painful?

Most exams cause only mild discomfort. We ensure you're comfortable throughout the process.

How often should I get a check-up?

An annual check-up is recommended for most women, more frequently if you have ongoing conditions.
