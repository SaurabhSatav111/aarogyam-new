Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Aarogyam Hospital. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through our website https://aarogyamhospitalsambhajinagar.com/. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and safeguard your information whenever you visit our website, submit enquiries, book appointments, or use our healthcare services. By accessing our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Age
  • Gender
  • City
  • Appointment Details
  • Health-related information voluntarily submitted through enquiry or appointment forms.

Technical Information

When you browse our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP Address
  • Browser Type
  • Device Information
  • Operating System
  • Date and Time of Visit
  • Pages Visited
  • Referral Website
  • Cookies and Analytics Data

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Schedule medical appointments
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Contact patients regarding appointments
  • Improve patient services
  • Improve website functionality
  • Maintain hospital records
  • Send important healthcare updates
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Protect against fraud and misuse

Medical Information

Any medical information submitted through our website is treated with strict confidentiality.

Medical information is only used for:

  • Appointment scheduling
  • Preliminary consultation
  • Patient communication
  • Healthcare service delivery

Our website should not be considered a substitute for professional medical consultation.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your information may be shared only with:

  • Hospital doctors
  • Medical staff
  • Diagnostic laboratories
  • Insurance providers (where authorized)
  • Government authorities if legally required
  • Technology providers assisting in website operations

All third-party service providers are expected to maintain confidentiality.

Data Security

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards including:

  • Secure servers
  • SSL encryption
  • Access controls
  • Password-protected systems
  • Regular monitoring
  • Data backup procedures

Although we use commercially acceptable methods to protect your information, no online transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to:

  • Improve browsing experience
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Remember user preferences
  • Enhance website performance

Users may disable cookies through browser settings.

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide healthcare services
  • Meet legal requirements
  • Maintain medical records
  • Resolve disputes
  • Improve our services

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

Patient Rights

Subject to applicable laws, patients may request:

  • Access to their personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of personal data (where legally permitted)
  • Withdrawal of consent
  • Restriction of processing

Requests may be submitted using the contact information below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for patients of all age groups. For minors, information should be submitted only by parents or legal guardians.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Aarogyam Hospital reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updated versions will be published on this page with the revised effective date.
